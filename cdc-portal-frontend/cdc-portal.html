Why IIT (ISM) Dhanbad

A legacy of engineering excellence

Home to India's most specialised talent in mining, petroleum, earth sciences, and applied engineering — alongside world-class programmes in CSE, ECE, and management.

01 / Heritage
Established in 1926 — India's oldest technical institute
Nearly a century of academic rigour and industry partnerships. IIT (ISM) has shaped India's mining, steel, and energy sectors for generations.
02 / Programmes
Unique specialisations unavailable elsewhere
India's only institute offering Petroleum Engineering, Mining Machinery, Applied Geophysics, and Earthquake Science at undergraduate and postgraduate levels.
03 / Diversity
32+ programmes across engineering, science, and management
Recruit from B.Tech, M.Tech, M.Sc, MBA (Business Analytics, Finance, Marketing), and Ph.D programmes — all under one roof, one campus visit.
04 / Rigour
JEE Advanced, GATE, JAM, and CAT selectivity
Every student has cleared a highly competitive national entrance examination, ensuring demonstrated academic merit and problem-solving ability.
05 / Industry Linkage
Deep ties with PSUs, MNCs, and startups alike
ONGC, Coal India, Tata Steel, Infosys, Goldman Sachs, and 500+ companies have a longstanding relationship with our placement office.
06 / Compliance
NIRF, RTI, and AIPC compliant recruitment process
Structured, transparent, and fully documented placement process aligned with national guidelines and regulatory requirements — zero ambiguity.
