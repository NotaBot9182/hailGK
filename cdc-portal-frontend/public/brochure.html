About The Institute

A Legacy of Excellence

One of India's oldest and most prestigious engineering institutions, IIT (ISM) Dhanbad has been advancing knowledge and innovation since 1926. With its elevation to IIT status in 2016, the institute has strengthened its commitment to academic excellence and research.

We combine decades of institutional heritage with cutting-edge research capabilities, creating an environment where students and faculty push the boundaries of engineering, science, and technology.

Our mission is to cultivate talented minds, foster groundbreaking research, and contribute meaningfully to India's development through excellence in education and innovation.
